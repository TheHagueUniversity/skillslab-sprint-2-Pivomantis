Màntas

Lithuanian DJ based in the Hague. Màntas is influenced by the old school Romanian techno scene and nu-disco originating from Lithuania & the Netherlands. Màntas implements elements of the new and the old to his mixes, in order to create something unique. Following the first Happening. party, his fanbase grew fast and is growing hungry for more appearances from this promising upcoming DJ
